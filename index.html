Akan Names

Hey,Find out your Akan Name

Akan names are derived from the Ghanian culture.
Children in Ghana are given their first name as a 'day name',which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
Here are the Ghanian day names:

MALE
  • Sunday:Kwasi
  • Monday:Kwadwo
  • Tuesday:Kwabena
  • Wednesday:Kwaku
  • Thursday:Yaw
  • Friday:Kofi
  • Saturday:kwame
FEMALE
  • Sunday:Akosua
  • Monday:Adwoa
  • Tuesday:Abenaa
  • Wednesday:Akua
  • Thursday:Yaa
  • Friday:Afua
  • Saturday:Ama
